Travel Estimator

There are three tabs below - origin and destination, Fuel consumption and Precision.

Origin and destination - set these two points by cpcking on map. You can choose more than two points!

Fuel consumption - insert value of fuel consumption of your vehicle (per 100 km). Maximum value of input is 100.

Precision - choose one of possible values (1, 2 or 3). The higher the value the better the precision. Higher precision may took longer to show results!

Input type number when user insert fuel consumption of his vehicle (l / 100 km).

Range picker (3 options) for precision of result.
